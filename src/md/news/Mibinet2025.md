Mibinet Aachen Day brought together researchers from HHU Düsseldorf, Forschungszentrum Jülich, the University of Cologne, and Bielefeld for a full day of scientific exchange in Aachen. The seminar featured research talks by Tanvir, Andreas, and Pascal, followed by lively discussion and networking across institutions.

Participants also had the chance to visit the iAMB laboratories, offering a closer look at facilities and ongoing work. After the seminar, the group continued the day with a relaxed visit to the Aachen Christmas market, enjoying the festive atmosphere and closing the event on a cheerful note.
