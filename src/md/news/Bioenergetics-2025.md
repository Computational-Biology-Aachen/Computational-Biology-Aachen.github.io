Several Lab members attended the Perspectives in Bioenergetics 2025 conference at Schloss Rauischholzhausen fromt the 29th of September to the 1st of October. At the conference Anna Matuszyńska gave a lecture titled "Integrative hybrid modelling of photosynthesis to quantify the biotechnological potential of cyanobacteria". The PhD students Quang Huy Nguyen and Josha Ebeling both partook in presenting their scientific posters.
